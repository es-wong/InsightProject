Your custom font palette

This project was completed in three weeks as a part of the Insight Data Science Fellowship.

Fontastic Four is a tool that helps burgeoning graphic designers select stylistically consistent font palattes.

Using transfer learning techniques, Fontastic Four recommends potential font pairings based on a balance of visual contrast and similiarity of the font families.
